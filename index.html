Some restaurants can be overrated as most of the time the rating is a global grade. Thus, you can have your worst dish ever in a restaurant which is considered as very good. Our solution is allowing customers to rate the specific dish they order at a restaurant using a smartphone app.

How does it work ?
  1. You connect to the app
  2. You are geo-localized. Therefore the app knows at which restaurant you are
  3. You take a picture of your dish
  4. You look for its name with the search option or enter a new name
  5. You give a score from 1 to 5 stars (1=No thanks! ; 5=Absolutely Delicious!Yummy Yummy Yumm!)

Do you consider yourself as a second class citizen or are you looking for the best things in life ? Imagine you are in holidays in this new frenzy city. Of course, you bought this 200 pages travel guide to get the most of your stay. Some decent restaurants are certainly mentioned in your guide. However, do you consider yourself as the basic tourist or would you like to enjoy a wonderful experience ? BestInTown is the app which brings you to the places where you will taste the best dishes. Are hungry for a 'Coq au vin' in Paris ? We know the restaurant where you can enjoy it. Bon appetit!
